Somehow this one floated under the radar and it’s definitely worth your time, to marvel at the unbelievable real-life story of Diana Nyad, her impossible resilience, and to bask in the glow of two of our greatest actors: Annette Bening (who plays Nyad) and Jodie Foster (her best friend and coach). This is a powerful movie about human will and our ability to accomplish physical feats that defy common opinion. You could search and find out what happens before you watch it, but I suggest you don’t — let the intensity grip you, let the emotional weight take you in, all amidst the beautiful complexities of a lifelong friendship.
