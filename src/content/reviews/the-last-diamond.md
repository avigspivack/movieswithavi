I am a sucker for Yvan Attal, so take this review with a grain of French salt - I will watch and enjoy pretty much anything that he’s in (happy to provide you with some of my faves if you’re interested). This film is a slick, fun, and well-paced mystery, and even though you probably know what’s going to happen, it almost doesn’t matter because the fun of it is how you get there. Attal plays a lifelong criminal named Simon who loves to con and steal; his “mark” is a serious diamond that is coming on public display and (of course) is under heavy guard. The witty and trustworthy Simon slips his way into being trusted, and the playful plot twists keep us smiling until the end.
