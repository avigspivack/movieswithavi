This film is a powerhouse. It takes place mostly in Copenhagen and on the palatial estate where the wedding (of the title) occurs; and the early scenes in India are both raw and gentle. Susanne Bier’s directing is stunning. It’s also a tough one to put into words, to describe the emotional gravity, and mostly the camera does the talking, staying close and holding you there (and it was so good that they made an American version). Definitely one of the best films I’ve seen in recent years.
