Thanks to my awesome daughter for this recommendation; she saw this one on a recent flight and then we all watched it together. I just love movies where you find yourself rooting for the criminals. In this case, it’s a fairly harmless crime because it’s just money, and as we meet the masterminds behind the crime, we recognize these are pot-smoking revolutionaries, nothing like Neil McCauley’s crew in “Heat.” These guys have no intention of getting caught nor of hurting anyone. So the stakes are lower and the humor is high. Get it?
