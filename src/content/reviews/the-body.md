I seriously regret the fact that it took me this long to see this movie. After watching and re-watching one of my all-time favorites, Oriol Paulo’s “The Invisible Guest,” this is the one he made two years earlier. You can totally see the similarities, though this one is darker (the whole thing seems to take place at night), and it’s constantly raining (cue “Seven” fans). You get sucked in deeper and more intensely until the climactic ending when you can’t actually believe the trick he’s pulled on you. No spoilers here, just go and rent it!
