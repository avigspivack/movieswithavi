A very impressive debut from Indian filmmaker, Ritesh Batra. He manages to take a small story and elevate it, using the oldest communication device: the hand-written letter, which is sent back and forth between the main characters through the lunch containers. As soon as this one ended, I wanted to watch it all over again. Beautifully made.
