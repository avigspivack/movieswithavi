Yes, I just watched this one again. And yes, I am back again with the OG and best buddy movie ever made - Newman and Redford, George Roy Hill, and quite simply a perfect (and surprisingly quirky) film. I have seen this one too many times to count, and every time I pick up something new - from the witty, William Goldman dialogue, to the almost-out-of-place musical interludes, to the glorious, modern cinematography. And Katharine Ross is sublime, too.
