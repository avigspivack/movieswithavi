This is one of my all-time French favorites. A perfectly paced film - it feels like you’re in constant motion - with an unexpected story; it also carries the deep weight of love. Since it’s originally based on a Harlan Coben story, the deep truths of the past play a central role in the reveal. Francois Cluzet is excellent in the lead role, and he probably runs a collective marathon during the course of the action. Don’t miss this one, and pay attention!
