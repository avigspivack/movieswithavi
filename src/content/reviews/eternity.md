Just when I thought Hollywood didn’t know how to make smart romcoms with just enough depth to not be shallow, along came this movie, which has a throwback feel, not a mobile device in sight, just some good old-fashioned romance. And the whole film is driven by the ultimate choice: Who will the wonderful Joan choose as her partner for eternity? We saw the trailer numerous times in theatres before we watched it, so we pretty much knew the premise - the key, then, was would it bring anything unexpected to capture some deeper intrigue or truth. Spoiler alert, it does, and I awoke the next morning with a satisfying feeling - not just that the ending makes you feel good, but that it felt earned. For a story that could have veered into bland, this one keeps you guessing just enough and lands you softly, right where you want to be.
