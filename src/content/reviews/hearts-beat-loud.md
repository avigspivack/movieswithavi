This one came back to me recently, probably because we are flying over to see our daughter who is studying abroad in Spain. And yes, I am total sucker for father/daughter movies, and this one absolutely nails it. Nick Offerman is spot on, Ted Danson as his bartender BFF is both cliched and just right, Toni Collette is always great, and I just learned that Kiersey Clemons performs the songs and delivers a pitch-perfect role! I’ve never been to Red Hook but it made me want to visit and hang out. This may not be a grand movie, but it has huge heart and humor.
