Don’t let the 3.5 hour runtime scare you away; this is a must-see film that I literally cannot stop thinking about. This is a HUGE movie that encompasses so many themes with such power, intensity, wisdom, and beauty - I was shocked to learn that writer/director Brady Corbet is both quite young and this is only his third feature (which he made for less than $10m!!). It represents Adrien Brody’s greatest performance, and it wraps itself around you such that you don’t want it to let go. The allusions you might read to “Citizen Kane” are real; it is that good, not perfect, but so ambitious that its flaws are forgiven in favor of the journey. And the destination.
