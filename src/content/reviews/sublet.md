This is a delicious, warm, and honest movie, and it makes you want to go to Tel-Aviv Israel immediately. The story centers around a middle-aged guy who shows up in Israel seemingly excepting his tourist trip to unfold in a certain way, and then - like life usually does - it goes sideways on him, especially after he meets the much younger and attractive Tomer. Written and directed by Israeli Eytan Fox, “Sublet” is a feast with heart, and it hits many human notes but without cliches or melodrama. Definitely a tasty watch for any evening.
