There are a few actors who I will watch in pretty much anything and Jessica Chastain is one of them. Both she and the excellent Eddie Redmayne give truly incredible performances; they carry the film along, and that's somewhat necessary because you pretty much know how this will all play out. But you don't know how. From the same Danish director who co-created "Another Round" and "The Hunt" (also highly recommended), this is definitely worth your time, but only if you're ready for a very intense narrative to unfold.
