I wish I hadn’t seen the trailer before we decided to watch the movie in full because this thrilling story is essentially told in two parts with just enough missing that you are genuinely guessing what will unfold. So my advice is to trust me on this one, don’t watch the trailer, and let the action unravel until you’re able to piece together what has transpired. This is not necessarily a “deep” movie, but it pulls at the universal themes of love and trust, and ultimately, the highly perceptive nature of women.
