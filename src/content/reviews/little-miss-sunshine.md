This might be one of the funniest and delightful movies you ever see. Absolutely brilliantly original, with a super sharp script, and a cast of characters you’d think would not be able to coexist, but in the hands of a roster of great actors - Toni Collette, Greg Kinnear, Abigail Breslin (the remarkable Olive), Paul Dano, Steve Carrell (what!!?), and the iconic Alan Arkin (RIP) - I mean, this is magic. It’s a comedy, dramedy, and road trip movie all wrapped into one. It has heart, rawness, and an incredible soundtrack. And if you don’t have a huge smile on your face as the final scene hits, then you don’t have a soul. And after I just wrote this, I think it’s time for another rewatch.
