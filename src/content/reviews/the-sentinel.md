Sometimes you just need a fun watch, and this is a good one, especially inside the White House. Michael Douglas always delivers, and Kiefer Sutherland does his gruffy best to keep up, as they twist and tangle on their way to uncovering the truth about the mole. This one is just so you don’t think I only watch highbrow movies with luscious cinematography - because there’s an art to making a movie that’s just enjoyable, and you can kick back and not worry about the gaping plot holes.
