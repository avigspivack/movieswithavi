I am biased because I am a sucker for films shot in black & white, especially those in NYC (see “Manhattan”). But in the hands of the deft Mike Mills (his “20th Century Women” was also excellent), this seemingly simple but layered story about the complexities of family relationships - especially with kids - has major repercussions, and its pitch-perfect acting performances make it a surprising must watch.
