Bravo for the chemistry between a young, feisty Margot Robbie and the pre-slap, hit the one note Will Smith. They make this movie work, even if the plot and the gags aren’t always consistent. Stay with it, because I really applaud the ending, which I did not see coming. In fact, it keeps you guessing a few times, uncertain who is playing who and when their actual feelings for each other may get in the way of their con work.
