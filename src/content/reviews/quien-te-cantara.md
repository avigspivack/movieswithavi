The literal translation of the title is “Who will sing to me?” and I am still thinking about this movie many days later. Director Carlos Vermut has created a deeply layered and gorgeously textured film. Yes, it explores celebrity and class, but in unexpected ways. There is so much subtlety to the incredible and delicate acting, the nuances of light and color, the unspoken words, and the crashing waves of the ocean - the fifth and uncredited character, who we know is always there.
