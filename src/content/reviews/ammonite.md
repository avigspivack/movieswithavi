I didn’t know this film was loosely based on some version of truth - as Anning and Murchison did know each other. But the passion that develops between them would have been shunned (and has been criticized). It is the utterly incredible chemistry between the actresses (Kate Winslet and Saoirse Ronan) that lifts this film to its heights. Otherwise, it moves so very slowly, it’s amidst the gray ocean waves, inching towards its beautiful climax.
