When you learn that this story actually happened - and dig up the details - it becomes both more amazing and harrowing. Mathieu is a cultural attaché in Siberia, thinking that the move from France will be good for his family and country. But what unfolds is a plot to imprison him, and we learn what an ordinary man is capable when he’s fighting for his survival. Gilles Lellouche is excellent in the lead role, and - despite a few unrealistic moments - the pace is fast and the tension high.
