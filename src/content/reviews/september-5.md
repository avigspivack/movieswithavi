I didn’t like this movie as much as I expected. I appreciated the retelling of a vital historical moment through the view of the ABC team and what happened behind the scenes. It was also commendable that they were able to condense the horrific 22-hour saga into roughly 90 minutes. But there was something missing; perhaps it was the lack of depth, or the fact that the center of the story is really the Israeli hostages who were murdered partially due to the ineptitude of the local German police - this was a different story, of proud journalists grappling with the moment and doing their best to share the story with the world. And in that, they succeeded.
