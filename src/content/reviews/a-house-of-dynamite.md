Sometimes movies disappoint you, and this is one of those times. And I can’t just have a blog to share what I think you should watch, but also perhaps what you should avoid; I’m not saying don’t watch this movie, but I am saying be forewarned that it did not live up to my expectations. Of course, when you’re Kathryn Bigelow - director of the excellent “Zero Dark Thirty” and the all-time classic original “Point Break” - the expectations are high, they should be high. So here we go: I guessed the ending halfway through, and that’s usually not a good sign. In this case, Bigelow has rolled out a pretty serious and recognizable cast (like all B+ folks) and she plays with the storytelling timeline, good enough tension… but it kinda ends there. Let’s just say that if our response to a nuclear missile threat is on par with what transpires here, we are in trouble.
