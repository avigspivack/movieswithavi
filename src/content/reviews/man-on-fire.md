Just re-watched this one on a recent flight, and no surprise, it holds up even after the tenth time; and that might be the thing about Tony Scott movies - even though he made them 20+ years ago, like this one (2004), they somehow still feel modern. With his trademark texture and jumpcut style, this is one of my all-time favorite action movies and Denzel performances. The thing that I recognized this time, is that Dakota Fanning - who was NINE during filming - gives one of the great overlooked child performances, and I put it up there with Haley Joel in “Sixth Sense.” And the chemistry between her and Denzel is quite special. And that’s the beauty of this movie - Scott doesn’t rush it, he slowly unravels the story, deepens the relationship between Lupita and Creasy, and then he takes it up 10x. Buckle up.
