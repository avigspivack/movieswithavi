There are many movies that should never be remade and this is one of them (in fact, the only great remake I can think of is “The Thomas Crown Affair”). So putting the remake aside, please don’t bother, this is such a badass, quotable movie from Kathryn Bigelow, I could watch it over and over (and I have). Early Keanu and Swayze in epic roles, Gary Busey as the veteran partner, and pre-internet, pre-crazy jump cutting action movie set in LA where surfing is a main character. Um yes! Throw in one of the best foot chase scenes ever filmed through the back alleys and homes of LA… pure gold. Hits every note with bravado and will live in my canon of the best 90s movies forever. Also the ending.
