I think we saw this French gem 20 years ago, and I can still recall it, including many of its humorous details. You don’t know where the director is taking you, and then you can’t believe what transpires; it’s an utterly shocking and delightful experience, and I highly recommend it. (It’s kinda like a superior, French version of “The Gift.”)
