Merci beaucoup a mon ami for the recommendation; based solely on the premise, I’m not sure we would have chosen to watch it, but I am so glad we did. Famed French actor Gilles Lellouche tries his hand behind the camera and directs this often hilarious and always full of heart film about a group of middle-aged men who form the local community synchronized swimming team, for which they receive ridicule. And despite (or because of!?) their respective life struggles, they find a place of belonging together, support each other (mostly), and find their way in and out of the pool. A beautiful story.
