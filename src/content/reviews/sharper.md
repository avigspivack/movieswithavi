Don’t be fooled by the trailer - this is a solid, old-fashioned con movie. It doesn’t hold a candle to the king of all con movies - “The Sting” - but that’s an impossible measuring stick. Instead, it’s right up there with the Will Smith flick “Focus,” which I also enjoyed immensely. The narrative structure is a straight rip / influence of Tarantino (you will notice the homage to “Pulp Fiction” and a love of movies in general). And I loved the pace - it eases you in, doesn’t rush things, and you think you know where it’s going just enough, that you know the characters and their motivations, and then it slips you into a new realm; I was genuinely, out loud shocked by the first big reveal. I hope you will be, too, because it will be much more fun to let yourself get sucked in without expecting much more.
