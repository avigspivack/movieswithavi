You probably haven’t heard of this film, made by Olivier Assayas, and starring the renowned Juliette Binoche and a fantastic Kristen Stewart. And I must say that a title does matter - this movie takes place in a real place called Sils Maria (in Switzerland) and its stunning natural beauty and mysterious clouds lay the foundation for this mesmerizing and enigmatic story. You don’t really know what to expect when it opens, with the duo traveling by train to an event; but as the narrative unfolds and blossoms its layers of depth, you realize the themes that have slowly emerged - of self-confidence, fame, the subjectivity of art, muses, and how we mature through our lives. It is a stunning movie, pensive and also always moving ahead, just as the clouds float through the valley.
