This is a beautiful movie, small in its characters but huge in heart and wisdom. When the enigmatic and worldly agent moves in upstairs, an older couple is smitten - they compete for his attention and are also internally awakened to take bold steps in their lives. There is a strain of humor throughout, but there are also striking scenes of courage and honesty and deep love. The ending is perfectly orchestrated. The three leads are all fantastic, and if you’re a fan of Israeli shows and movies like I am, you will recognize them from “Shtisel” and “Foxtrot.” Just remember not to fear the microphone.
