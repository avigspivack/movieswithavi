Okay, okay, sometimes you just need mindless and fun watch because it’s mindless and fun, and it’s just fun enough that you’re entertained. I have enjoyed Keira Knightley since her early work in “Bend it Like Beckham” (a very fun one that you should definitely see). Over the years she has shown some impressive range, but I will warn you that this Netflix flick is not one of them. And that’s okay. She’s just good enough, and the plot is just good enough to keep you sort of guessing, and the running time is just right so you don’t actually get bored. Keep your expectations in check, and you will enjoy yourself!
