The word “gett” in Hebrew refers to the document for a divorce. In Jewish tradition, a divorce is a literal un-doing of a marriage, and the husband must grant this permission to his wife. That context sets the stage for this powerhouse of a film, which feels more like a theatre piece, but you cannot look away - and it may stir up all kinds of emotion.
