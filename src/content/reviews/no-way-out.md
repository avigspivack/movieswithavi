Well they definitely don’t make them like this anymore! This is a late 80s gem, complete with a great Gene Hackman performance (RIP), and a young, slim, on the run Kevin Costner, handsome as ever in those Navy whites. You will laugh at the computer technology, the prominence of corded and pay phones, and the cheesy but thematic music. But pay attention to the end because the payoff is worth it.
