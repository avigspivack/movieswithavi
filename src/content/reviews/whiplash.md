I saw this film on a plane and now I need to rewatch it because I’m sure the sound was way better than I experienced it. I also can’t believe I hadn’t seen it until now because I love the films of Damien Chazelle (“La La Land,” “First Man”). I will just say that the title is reflective of how you feel after watching it. This movie has electric energy, it’s brilliantly acted (Miles Teller and JK Simmons - who won the Oscar - are incredible), and despite the sometimes painful intensity, you can’t stop watching (nor should you because the ending is pitch perfect).
