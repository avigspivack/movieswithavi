I am a little embarrassed to admit I hadn’t seen this one before. But I am excited to share that we watched it on a rooftop in Miami, so that was cool. So much to love here - early Julia Stiles and Joseph Gordon-Levitt, and then oh what could have been with the great Heath Ledger in the male lead (w magnetic charisma), a sharp & witty script, and a cameo by Allison Janney as a writerly guidance counselor. Within the canon of great high school flicks - John Hughes’ legendary work and “Say Anything” - this one slides in just under the best of those, though some may argue it belongs in their rarified air. And especially for a Shakespearean guy like me, I enjoy the flowing sonnets; and even though you know exactly where love will eventually blossom, that’s most of the fun of it anyway.
