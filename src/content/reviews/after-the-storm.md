What do they say about pizza? Even when it’s bad, it’s still pretty good. Well, this film from one of my favorite directors (Kore-ada, see “Broker” and “Shoplifters”) is not at all bad - it’s just not one of his greats — and it’s still better than most movies you’ll see this year. As with all of his films, the story is simple and revolves around the dynamics of family (and the repeated refrain from several characters is something along the lines of “my life wasn’t supposed to end up this way”). As another typhoon approaches, we observe the closely detailed and intimate interactions of the family unfold, waiting waiting for what will happen… after the storm.
