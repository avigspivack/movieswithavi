I realize I am breaking all the rules here (kind of like this show), as I write about a non-movie on a movie blog. But after finishing the third season, and being blown away, I felt compelled to share. This is probably my favorite show of all time; it has incredible energy, humor, remarkable and quotable characters, a deep dark intense premise, and its soul is pure - all about how we humans seek greatness, even if we are afraid to pursue it. I could go on for pages about this show, and maybe I will in a different format, but for now suffice it to say that you must watch it.
