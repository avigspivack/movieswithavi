I once engaged in a passionate verbal dispute about the brilliance of this film’s last scene - one of my all-time favorites. This is a film made by and starring an Italian who loves food (Stanley Tucci); its heart is big, its soundtrack by Louis Prima, and - like life - it’s messy, unexpected, uncertain. But worth every bite. (see what I did there?)
