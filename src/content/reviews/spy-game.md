RIP Tony Scott - this guy really knew how to make fast-paced, engaging, and smooth films, and this is one of my top ten favorite rewatchables. It has everything: an electric premise, great cast, perfect use of flashbacks, and a nice blend of action - both in CIA conference rooms and in the field. Scott’s camera flies around buildings, he unleashes huge explosions, and he still manages to blend in just enough sentimentality so that you care about the father-son dynamic between Redford and Pitt. If you haven’t seen this one, don’t hesitate; I’m about to go watch it again.
