I was surprised to discover that I hadn’t reviewed this film yet because it’s in my upper echelon all time. We recently rewatched it with our daughter - who was seeing it for the first time - and I was reminded why. Loosely based on the Mossad capture of Adolf Eichmann, the story jumps between past and present, daring us to determine what will happen, a physical scar and a deep secret suppressed for three decades. With Jessica Chastain and Helen Mirren in the co-lead role, the acting is obviously superb. Toss in a little Sam Worthington and Tom Wilkinson, and we are off. Don’t hesitate, because you will always need to pay your debts, whatever they are.
