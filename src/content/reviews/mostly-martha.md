The trailer for this film makes it seem like a light-hearted foodie romantic comedy, but it’s grossly misrepresented. This is a movie about love - of our family, of our craft - and it is beautifully told, steering clear of cliche. It was remade in an American version with Catherine Zeta-Jones (“No Reservations”), but this original is far superior. I highly recommend it for a warm evening in.
