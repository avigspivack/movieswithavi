I may have cracked double digits in the number of times I’ve watched this one. It’s like the “modern western buddy film” where the brothers use stolen cars instead of horses to rob enough to pull their family out of poverty. The Taylor Sheridan script is fantastic, and David Mackenzie’s direction is spot on, particularly the perfectly attuned soundtrack. One of Jeff Bridges’ best and most underrated performances. Make sure you watch to the very last frame.
