We could all take a lesson from the ageless Clint (Eastwood); the guy is 94 and counting, he’s won multiple Oscars, and he’s still creating - releasing movies that still hold up, even if he’s not acting in them anymore. This is another solid one, and great for a recent flight because it moves at a swift pace and you’re not quite sure how it will end (even though you think you know how it should). I will also see anything with Toni Collette - one of the more underrated actors we have (she’s excellent as the nameless small town DA). Don’t let the bland title fool you, this is a very worthwhile watch. And I can’t believe I knew the lead Nicholas Hoult from the long-ago Hugh Grant film “About a Boy.” Takes me all the way back.
